// NEED TO KNOW

FREQUENTLY ASKED

WHAT IS CYPHER?+
CYPHER is an inter-school tech and puzzle event by the Cypher Club of Delhi Public School. A full-day challenge with a flagship cryptography event and 6 sub-events across multiple venues — testing logic, creativity, and teamwork.
WHO CAN PARTICIPATE?+
CYPHER is open to all schools! Any student from any school can register and compete. Designed for participants across different grade levels. Bring your crew and represent your school.
HOW DO I REGISTER?+
Registration is completely FREE. Click the Register button or follow @cypherdps on Instagram for the link and updates. Slots are limited — register early!
WHAT ARE THE TEAM SIZE RULES?+
All events are team-based. Team sizes vary per event — details will be shared closer to the date via Instagram and the registration portal. Stay tuned!
WHAT ARE THE PRIZES?+
Winners and runners-up receive trophies and certificates. Every participant gets a participation certificate. Top performers in the main CYPHER event are eligible for the grand trophy.
