// NEED TO KNOW

FREQUENTLY ASKED

WHAT IS (c)ync v13?+
(c)ync v13 is a gaming event organized by the Cypher Club of Delhi Public School. A full-day inter-school gaming extravaganza featuring the flagship challenge and 6 sub-events across multiple venues — testing skill, strategy, and teamwork.
WHO CAN PARTICIPATE?+
Open to all schools! Any student from any school can register and compete. Designed for participants across different grade levels. Bring your squad and represent your school.
HOW DO I REGISTER?+
Registration is completely FREE. Click "Join the Game" or follow @cypherdps on Instagram for the link and updates. Slots are limited — register early!
WHAT ARE THE TEAM SIZE RULES?+
All events are team-based. Team sizes vary per event — details will be shared closer to the date via Instagram and the registration portal.
WHAT ARE THE PRIZES?+
Winners and runners-up receive trophies and certificates. Every participant gets a participation certificate. Top performers in (c)ync v13 main event are eligible for the grand trophy.
